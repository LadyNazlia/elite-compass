Annual Care Giver Retreat

Professional Care Givers! "Reflect, Restore, Renew."

A.C.G.R.

Caregiving, is one of the most stressful tasks that anyone can take on. As a provider of care, caregiversare responsible for the wellbeing of those who would lack much needed support, without the help of the caregiverthose needing assistance would experienceillness, pain, and stress. Thatâ€™s a big job.

Yet, in order to care for someone else, one must effectively take time to care for oneself. You must take time to Reflect, Restoreand Renew. This is done by stepping away from a busy schedule and taking a breath of fresh air.

The Annual Care Giver Retreat is to give pause for the busy professional caregiver to reflect on her/his gift of caregiving, restore the soul so that he/she may renew the compassionate spirit needed to care for another person effectively.
