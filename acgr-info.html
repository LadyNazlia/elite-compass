Annual Care Giver Retreat

Professional Care Givers! "Reflect, Restore, Renew."

A.C.G.R.

As a caregiver, you give a lot of your time to others to make sure their daily needs of comfort are provided.

Have you taken care of yourself lately? Care giving, is one of the most stressful tasks that anyone can take on. As a provider of care, you are responsible for the well-being of those who would lack many needed services, services that may cause illness, pain, and stress. That’s a big job.

Yet, in order to care for someone else, one must effectively take time to care of oneself. You must take time to Reflect, Renew and Restore your own emotional vitality. This is done by stepping away from a busy schedule and taking a breath of fresh air.

YOU are invited to attend ‘The Annual Care Giver Retreat’ with other caregivers just like yourself. All attendees will be given an agenda that will be the task of Reflection, so that each may Restore his/her weary spirits and be involved in Recreating the attitude of peace, purpose, and hope.

This Year's Theme:
Mardi Gras


Why Mardi Gras?

Allowing the customs, historical trends, and familiar outfits to set the scene for tired care givers to reflect, restore and recreate a renewed sense of calling to your gift.

Come dressed in your best Mardi Gras costume.

A time known to all for celebrating, frolicking and, pleasure. Mardi Gras is a time of living it up and having fun. The Tuesday before Ash Wednesday prepares many for the Season of Lent and the celebration of the resurrection. Will you be able to let your hair down and join others in the community gathering of the year?

The Retreat will be held by Chaplain Horace Cutter, M.Div., BCPC. Chaplain Cutter and the other spiritual caregivers will lead the caregivers retreat in activities, reflective readings and times of solitude that will bring opportunities for personal reflection, restoration, and renewal.

ACGR will be among beautiful scenery at Summer Grove Town Square Bldg Newnan, Ga to soothe the soul and quiet your spirit. The Retreat will begin with breakfast & welcome 8:30 am – 9:00 am.
